Information about the river:

The Hudson River is a river spanning the west side Manhattan. It runs four miles along Manhattan's west side attracting over 17 million visits each year. Additionally, it provides an oasis for New Yorkers and visitors alike with a variety of recreational and educational activities. The park is the largest open space in Manhattan since the completion of Central Park. People cosider it to be a beloved, urban recreational paradise.

Hudson River Park is where you go in NYC for recreation, relaxing, events, culture, dining, panoramic views and more.
